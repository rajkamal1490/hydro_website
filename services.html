Services in Expertise

Our projects division handles complex and specialized projects covering civil, mechanical, electrical and instrumentation activities involving design & engineering, procurement, construction management, pre-commissioning and commissioning of the projects within the stipulated time period.

System design & engineering consultancy

We help our clients to design the system by making case studies and understanding the actual requirements of the site in the past, present and future by appointing experts who can handle their compliance of various statutes that apply to them based on the nature and scale of their operations.

Pump Repair, Over Hauling and Retro Fitting

We have the unique ability to repair pumps using our pump reverse engineering expertise, welding, machining, seal repair, and existing parts catalog for a quick, efficient, and cost effective overhaul. Oftentimes, we upgrade materials of construction to increase service life.

Energy Management and Audits

Our main objective is to design energy efficient system and to promote best practices in energy management to both the public and private sectors. We study and understand the system and recommend organisations to start with an energy audit, which is a review of how and where energy is consumed in the organisation. Energy audits help identify Energy Management Opportunities where savings of energy and money can be made.
